### Design Specifications
- **Input Voltage**: 24V DC
- **Output Voltage**: 5V DC
- **Current Rating**: Up to 60A (with 60A fuse protection)
- **Protection Features**: 
  - Reverse polarity protection
  - Surge protection
  - EMI filtering

### Key Components
- Buck converter (24V to 5V)
- Protection circuits
- Filter capacitors
- Current sensing provisions
